Work for the lead Qld government agency driving affordable, reliable, clean energy through the Queensland Energy and Jobs Plan.

Working with Us 
- You'll be involved in work that is constantly evolving, inspired by the impact that we make, and thrive on the challenge of forging a strong future for Queensland
- Take responsibility for your career direction and development. Access formal and informal training, mentoring and development programs and support.

Your expertise 
You will be a strategic influencer - capable of connecting people, workstreams, themes and insights to bring new value and greater momentum towards the division's overarching objectives.
• Analyse issues in a critical and insightful manner and problem-solves to develop prompt, considered responses.
• Provide timely information and advice, tailors messages to stakeholder needs, and considers a diversity of perspectives to achieve mutually beneficial outcomes.
• Proven experience developing and interpreting quantitative models.
• Experience in complex data analysis using fit for purpose tools (e.g. Excel, Python, R, SQL).
• Presentation/data visualization skills such as Power BI.
• At least 5 years relevant experience in analytical roles.
• Experience/knowledge of the Energy sector would be highly regarded.
